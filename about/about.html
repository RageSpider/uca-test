Our Mission

The United Club Association (UCA) is dedicated to fostering a vibrant community of clubs at University Laboratory School and College. We aim to empower students by providing resources, guidance, and a platform for collaboration among clubs.

Through our efforts, we strive to create an environment where students can explore their interests, develop leadership skills, and contribute to the school's culture.

Our Core Values

  • Community

    We believe in building a strong sense of community among students, faculty, and staff.

  • Excellence

    We encourage excellence in all activities and initiatives undertaken by our clubs.

  • Inclusivity

    We are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all students, regardless of background or interests.
